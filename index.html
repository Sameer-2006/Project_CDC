Why Recruit @ IIT (ISM) Dhanbad


IIT (ISM) Dhanbad is one of India’s oldest technical institutions, known for its strong academic foundation and industry relevance. The institute offers a diverse pool of students trained in analytical thinking, problem-solving, and practical engineering application.

The Career Development Centre coordinates internships, projects, and placement initiatives, while students actively participate in technical clubs, competitions, and interdisciplinary activities.
